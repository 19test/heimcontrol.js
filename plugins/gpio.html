GPIO Plugin

Control the GPIO ports on your Raspberry PI

What it does

The GPIO plugin is able to control the GPIO ports of the Raspberry PI. It uses the great pi-gpio%nbsp;tool to access the GPIO pins of the Raspberry Pi.
